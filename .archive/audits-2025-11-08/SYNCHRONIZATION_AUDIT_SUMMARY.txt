Files with bare processEvents() calls:
  ✗ tests/unit/test_threede_controller_signals.py (8 instances)

  ✗ tests/unit/test_signal_manager.py (1 instance)
  ✗ tests/unit/test_actual_parsing.py (1 instance)

Issue: Bare `qapp.processEvents()` without condition-based waits
Risk: Tests may be flaky under high CPU load or slow systems
Fix: Wrap in qtbot.waitSignal() or qtbot.waitUntil()

EXAMPLE BEFORE:
  qapp.processEvents()
  time.sleep(0.1)  # Compensating for bare call
  qapp.processEvents()

EXAMPLE AFTER:
  with qtbot.waitSignal(signal, timeout=1000):
      trigger_operation()
  # No sleep needed - signal proves operation started
